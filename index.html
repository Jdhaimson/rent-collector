MyRent Pricing + Sign up

The rest of your life is online, shouldn't paying rent be?

Forget about writing checks. Pay your rent in seconds. MyRent works with any landlord, so you can pay rent easily.

Pay Any Landlord

Provide your landlord's email and address. We'll invite your landlord to receive payments through MyRent, or we'll mail them a check on your behalf!

Learn more
Select your preferred way of paying your landlord once your profile has been set up. Select the option of paying by check if you would like MyRent to send a check on your behalf to your landlord. With at least 3 days in advance, we will write a check for you, mail it to your landlord, and offer you notifications along the way. If your landlord would like to receive direct transfers instead, we will invite them to setup a landlord account with MyRent. With just a single day in advance, your landlord will directly receive your rent.

Seamless Payment Setup

Set up your payment profile in seconds by linking your bank account, PayPal, or credit card. That's it! From then on, securely pay rent quickly and easily from your laptop, phone, or tablet.

Learn more
On your request, we will securely withdraw funds from your account to pay your rent on time. Enter your account and routing number to link your bank account with MyRent. Alternatively, you can link your MyRent account with a PayPal account or credit card to withdraw funds from these accounts. An additional fee of 2.5% is added for credit card transactions.

Pay Rent Anytime

MyRent lets you make one-time payments, schedule a few payments, or set up automatic payments to never miss paying rent again!

Learn more
When you select a one-time payment, MyRent will withdraw funds from your account and begin your one-time transaction immediately. Ever forget to pay rent? MyRent allows you to set up recurring, automatic payments so you never have to worry about missing another payment again. Simply set the day of the month you'd like your landlord to receive your rent, and we will handle the rest.
